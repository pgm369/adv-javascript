Temperature Converter!

This program allows you to input a temperature in Celsius. It then effortlessly converts this Celsius temperature into Fahrenheit and presents the result in an informative sentence format. For instance, if you enter a temperature of X degrees Celsius, the program will display "X degrees Celsius is equivalent to Y degrees Fahrenheit." Here, X represents your initial Celsius input, while Y represents the converted Fahrenheit temperature, rounded to two decimal places.
